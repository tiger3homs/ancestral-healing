What this project is

The one-paragraph orientation

Ancestral Healing is a health & wellness media brand that produces educational video and short-form (Reels/Shorts) content for YouTube and social platforms. The archive is a working content-production library: research notes, video scripts, distilled podcast/lecture summaries, reel hooks, downloadable guides, and a competitor map. It is not a product or codebase — it is the raw material and outlines behind a channel. The editorial worldview is ancestral / animal-based / quantum-biology-adjacent, blending traditional-diet advocacy with circadian-light science and a non-toxic-living angle.

The editorial worldview

The recurring claims that unify the archive — presented as the brand's own positions

These are the load-bearing beliefs the content returns to again and again. Documenting them here is descriptive: it maps what the library argues, not an endorsement of the science. Several claims (raw-milk safety, sun/cancer, EMF/5G, deuterium, "cancer is mitochondrial") are contested or go beyond mainstream consensus — the raw-milk doc itself flags this.

Food is ancestralNUTRITION
Health comes from returning to what humans ate for millennia — quality animal fats, organs, seafood, seasonal produce — sourced locally from regenerative farms. Seed oils, refined sugar, grains and processed food are "the real culprits" behind chronic disease.
Plants can harmANTINUTRIENTS
Phytates, oxalates, lectins, tannins and enzyme/protease inhibitors block mineral absorption and irritate the gut lining — so many "superfoods" (kale, spinach, grains, legumes) are reframed as suboptimal.
Light is a nutrientCIRCADIAN
Morning sun, full-spectrum daylight and red/near-infrared light drive mitochondrial ATP; artificial blue light causes "malillumination," disrupts melatonin/dopamine and is framed as metabolically toxic.
Never miss a sunrisePROTOCOL
The single most-repeated actionable: view the rising sun with unshielded eyes within ~30 min of waking to anchor the circadian clock. "Act like the Sphinx in the morning; block blue light at night."
Mitochondria are centralMECHANISM
Health is framed as mitochondrial energy flux (electrons, protons, light, water, magnetism) over macronutrients. Cancer is described as "a mitochondrial disease, not genetic."
Environment > genesDECENTRALIZED
Grounding/earthing, cold exposure, EMF & 5G avoidance, and geographic/latitude choices recur. Strong "decentralized medicine," take-personal-responsibility, distrust-the-system framing.
The modern world is toxicNON-TOXIC
Microplastics, PFAS and BPA-family endocrine disruptors are tied to fertility, thyroid and hormone harm; glass and natural materials are the recommended defaults.
⚠ Handling note for anyone producing from this library

This archive mixes well-supported ideas (circadian rhythm matters; ultra-processed food and excess screen light are real concerns) with fringe or disputed claims (sun exposure preventing breast cancer, 5G causing cancer spikes, deuterium/"dyum" theory, DARPA/blue-light control narratives). Fact-check and add qualifiers before publishing, and keep the medical-disclaimer language the raw-milk and appendicitis docs already model.

The signature daily protocol

Distilled from “Circadian Daily Health Routine” + “Master Your Circadian”
06:00–10:00
Anchor the clock

Sunrise within 10–30 min of waking, no sunglasses. Red/NIR light 10–15 min. Cold water. Gentle movement + breathwork.

10:00–14:00
Live in daylight

Work in natural light or by a window. Functional movement breaks. Mindful, screen-free meals.

14:00–18:00
Ground & move

20+ min forest bathing, barefoot grounding. Restorative strength work. Emotional/"conflict" journaling.

18:00–21:00
Signal dusk

Sunset gazing or red light. Dim the home to candles/amber. Light meal. Unwind to release stress.

21:00–06:00
Full darkness

Blackout sleep, EMF/Wi-Fi off, blue-light blockers after dark. Visualization to close the day.
