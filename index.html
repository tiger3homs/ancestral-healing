Owner questions, answered

What are we actually buying ourselves?

What did we build?

An AH Brain knowledge base, channel analytics, an autopilot/workflow plan, this Netlify owner portal, and a searchable source explorer. Each layer is usable on its own; together they turn a scattered archive into an operating system.

What workflow does it create?

A daily intelligence brief and weekly content council feed fact-checking and script-writing skills, asset generation, and a publish checklist—with human approval before release. A six-week rollout makes the change manageable.

Why can this work?

The channel has an existing base, analytics show carnivore/animal-based themes work, and 20+ minute videos perform best. Strong nutrition documentation supplies depth; circadian/light material has a repackaging opportunity through stronger hooks.

What does it cost?

Plan for 15–25 setup hours over six weeks. Monthly tooling can start at about $92–155 to validate, with a recommended standard scenario around $283 and an aggressive scale scenario around $695.

Six-week workflow & rollout

Build the habit before turning up the volume.

The automation is deliberately staged so quality, safety, and voice can be tested before scale.

Week 1

Align

Confirm voice, claim boundaries, pillars, and approval rules.

Week 2

Connect

Set up the daily intelligence brief and source intake.

Week 3

Council

Run the weekly content council and score ideas against signals.

Week 4

Produce

Test fact-checking, scripting, and asset-generation handoffs.

Week 5

Publish

Use the checklist and owner approval gate on pilot videos.

Week 6

Review

Measure workload, quality, and results; adjust the cadence.
