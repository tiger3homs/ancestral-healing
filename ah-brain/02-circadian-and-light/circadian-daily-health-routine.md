# Circadian Daily Health Routine

**Circadian Daily Health Routine**

**MORNING (6:00 AM - 10:00 AM)**

* Sunrise Exposure: Step outside within 10-30 minutes of waking. No sunglasses. Resets melatonin/cortisol

balance.

* Red/NIR Light: 10-15 mins on chest or back. Stimulates healing mitochondria.

* Cold Water Therapy: Contrast shower or cold rinse. Activates blood flow and resilience.

* Gentle Movement & Emotional Check-in: Joint mobility + breathwork. Resolve freeze patterns.

**MIDDAY (10:00 AM - 2:00 PM)**

* Work in Natural Light: Walk outdoors or sit near a window.

* Functional Movement Break: Crawl, squat, walk to reset posture and joint flow.

* Mindful Eating: Grounded meals, without screen distractions. Support digestion.

* Optional NIR Spot Treatment: Use red light on tense areas like neck or low back.

**AFTERNOON (2:00 PM - 6:00 PM)**

* Forest Bathing: 20+ mins in green space. Ground barefoot when possible.

* Conflict Journal: Reflect on unresolved feelings to prevent psychosomatic storage.

* Restorative Movement: Deep squats or body weight training.

**EVENING (6:00 PM - 9:00 PM)**

* Red Light or Sunset Gazing: Signal body for melatonin release.

* Dimming the Home: Use candles or amber lights to mimic dusk.

* Light Meal

* Emotional Unwinding: Shake, breathe, journal, or pray to resolve inner conflict.

**NIGHT (9:00 PM - 6:00 AM)**

* Sleep in Full Darkness: Remove EMFs and noise sources.

* Visualization: Imagine healing light pulsing through your body-earth's rhythm flowing in.
