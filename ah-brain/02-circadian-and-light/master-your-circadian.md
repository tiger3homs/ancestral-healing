# Master Your Circadian

**Master Your Circadian Rhythm - For Free**

Expose yourself to natural sunlight within 30 minutes of waking - no sunglasses, no windows, just you and the light. This anchors your circadian rhythm.

Ground yourself daily. Barefoot contact with earth discharges built-up EMFs and harmonizes your nervous system.

Gaze gently at the rising and setting sun. This ancient practice recalibrates your hormonal clock.

Avoid artificial light after sunset. Replace LED and fluorescent bulbs with amber light, candles, or red lights.

Use blue light blockers on screens after dark. Even one hour of blue light at night can suppress melatonin.

Turn off Wi-Fi, phones, and electronics during sleep. EMFs disturb your natural electrical field and deep repair.

Sleep in full darkness. Use blackout curtains and remove any lights in your bedroom.

Spend time in nature and among trees. Phytoncides and natural sounds reduce cortisol and

stabilize your mood.

Eliminate toxic hygiene and cleaning products. Endocrine disruptors interfere with hormones like melatonin, cortisol, and thyroid.

Avoid fluoride, parabens, synthetic fragrance, and detergents. These chemicals shift your inner rhythm off course.

Resolve internal conflicts and stress patterns (Germanic New Medicine view). Unresolved stress disturbs the brain relay controlling your sleep and circadian repair.

Journal your energy, sleep, light exposure, and mood. The more aware you are, the more you align.

Honor moon phases and natural cycles. Your biology is lunar as well as solar.

Replace overhead lighting at night with soft lamps near floor level - mimic the firelight of our

ancestors. Use candle lighting or red light at night after sunsets.
