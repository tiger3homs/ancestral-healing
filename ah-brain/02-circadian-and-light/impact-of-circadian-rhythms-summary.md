# Impact of Circadian Rhythms on Health — bioSpectral (summary)

> **Third-party document.** Published by BioSpectral Systems ("Visible Light Hygiene").
> The original PDF is kept in `/source-pdfs/`. Summarized rather than reproduced.

## Core thesis

The body runs millions of physiological processes without conscious input — hormone
production, mitochondrial energy transfer, water generation — and these are not
random. They are timed by environmental cues, chiefly light. Circadian rhythm is
presented as the scheduling layer underneath all other health interventions.

## Key themes

- **Processes are scheduled, not spontaneous.** Hormone release, mitochondrial
  energy transfer and metabolic water generation each run on a clock keyed to
  environmental light cues.
- **Environmental cues set the clock.** Sunrise, daylight intensity, sunset and
  darkness are the primary zeitgebers; artificial light at the wrong hours
  desynchronizes the schedule.
- **Downstream consequences.** Disrupted timing is linked to metabolic, hormonal and
  sleep dysfunction — the same territory covered by the archive's own
  `master-your-circadian.md` and `circadian-daily-health-routine.md`.
