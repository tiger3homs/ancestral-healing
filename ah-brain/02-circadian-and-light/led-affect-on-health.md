### **A Silent Switch to LED: Who Benefits?**

Did you know incandescent light bulbs have been systematically banned in favor of LEDs? Governments worldwide championed the switch, touting LEDs as a solution for energy efficiency and environmental sustainability. But here’s the twist—what they didn’t tell us is how this “progress” might be sabotaging our health.

Jack Kruse doesn’t mince words on this: *“LEDs may save electricity, but they’re costing us our biology. The artificial blue light they emit disrupts our circadian rhythms, damages our mitochondria, and accelerates chronic disease.”*

(Overlay Kruse’s voice: *“Every time you turn on an LED, you’re bathing your body in a spectrum it was never designed to process. It’s not just artificial—it’s alien.”* Cut to visuals of brightly lit urban centers and a contrasting scene of natural, golden sunlight filtering through trees.)

What’s worse, this push to “modernize” our lighting wasn’t about human health. It was about cutting costs. The very institutions that should be safeguarding our well-being instead prioritized energy efficiency over biological compatibility, leaving us in a world where even our homes are a health hazard.

But here’s where it gets unsettling. Most of us live under an alien sun. Artificial light, from LEDs to our phone screens, bathes us in a spectrum our bodies weren’t designed to handle. And it’s not just a problem for our eyes—this blue light distorts our internal clocks, disrupts our hormones, and stretches the vital proteins in our cells, causing a cascade of energy inefficiency.
