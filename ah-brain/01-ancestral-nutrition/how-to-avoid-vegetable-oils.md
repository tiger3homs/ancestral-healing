# How to Avoid Vegetable Oils

> **Asset type:** A printable restaurant card + avoidance guide. Hand to restaurant
> staff or use as a downloadable lead magnet.

## Oils to avoid

- Canola oil
- Soybean oil
- Sunflower oil
- Corn oil
- Peanut oil
- Palm oil
- Cottonseed oil
- Safflower oil
- Grapeseed oil
- "Vegetable oil" (unspecified blends)
- Margarine / shortening

## Where they hide

Vegetable oils are used well beyond obvious frying. Watch for them in:

- Frying, sautéing, baking and roasting
- Sauces, seasonings, spices and marinades
- Pre-packaged and processed foods
- Oiling the grill to prevent sticking
- Salad dressings and dips

## What to request instead

Ask that food be cooked in one of these:

- Butter or ghee (grass-fed preferred)
- Beef tallow
- Lard or suet
- Coconut oil
- Extra-virgin olive oil (not for high heat)

## Restaurant script

> "I have a sensitivity to vegetable oils. Could my food be cooked in butter,
> olive oil, or tallow instead? Please also check that no oil is used on the grill."
