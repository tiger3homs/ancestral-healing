# Primal Nutrition — reference note

## What it is

A book-length treatment of Paleolithic and ancestral dietary patterns and their
application to modern health. It sits alongside the Weston A. Price tradition of
traditional-diets research that this archive repeatedly draws on.

## Why it's in the library

It is the closest thing the archive has to an academic-style anchor for the
Ancestral Nutrition pillar — useful when a script needs a book citation rather than
a podcast clip.

## How to use it

- Cite by title and author when a video makes a traditional-diets claim.
- Do **not** paste passages into scripts. Paraphrase and attribute.
- Pair with `ancestral-nutrition-for-lasting-health-and-wellbeing.md`, which is the
  archive's own manifesto version of the same argument.
