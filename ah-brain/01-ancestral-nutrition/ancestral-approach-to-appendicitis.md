## 🥩 1. Ancestral Nutrition (Nose-to-Tail Approach)

- High-quality animal foods only — anti-inflammatory, gut-friendly, nutrient-dense

## 🌞 3. Natural Therapies for Adhesions (External + Internal)

### 🔴 Red Light Therapy (NIR/IR)

- 10–20 minutes/day directly on abdomen

- Stimulates mitochondrial healing, reduces inflammation and scar tissue

### 🌿 Castor Oil Packs

- Apply castor oil over abdomen + heat pad (or under red light) for 30–45 mins

- 3–5x/week — improves lymph flow, breaks down fibrous tissue

### 👐 Manual Therapies

- **Visceral Manipulation** (find a trained practitioner, e.g. Barral Institute certified)

- **Myofascial Release** therapy to reduce fascial adhesions

- Gentle abdominal massage at home (clockwise circles, belly lifts)

### 🧘 Gentle Fascial Movement

- **Somatic stretching**: Cat/cow, spinal waves, hip circles

- **Walking**: Daily after meals (10–20 mins)

- **Breathwork**: Diaphragmatic (belly) breathing, especially lying down — 5 mins, 2–3x/day

  > Tip: Fascia is *viscoelastic* and responds best to slow, rhythmic movement and pressure
