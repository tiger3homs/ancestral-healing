# Carnivore Guide (Kevin Stock) — reference note

> **Third-party published guide — not reproduced.** A 79-page copyrighted guide,
> *The Ultimate 30-Day Guide to Going Full Carnivore* by Kevin Stock. No body text
> has been extracted into this repo.

## What it is

A structured 30-day carnivore-diet onboarding guide, framed as an "experiment"
rather than a permanent commitment — the pitch being that anyone can commit to
30 days.

## Why it's in the library

The archive's own manifesto notes that the brand's journey went **carnivore for two
years → animal-based ancestral**. This guide represents that first stage, and is the
reference for what a strict-elimination starting protocol looks like.

## How to use it

- As a structural model for building an *original* AH 30-day challenge or lead
  magnet — the format (30-day framing, staged onboarding) is the transferable part.
- Cite Kevin Stock by name when referencing the carnivore-elimination approach.
- Do **not** copy its content into AH guides. Build your own from the archive's
  own material (`animal-based-food-list.md`, `anti-nutrient-groups-and-foods-to-avoid.md`).
