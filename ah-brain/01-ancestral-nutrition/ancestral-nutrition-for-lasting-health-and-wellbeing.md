# Ancestral Nutrition for Lasting Health and Wellbeing

Ancestral Nutrition for Lasting Health and Wellbeing

For thousands of years, humans have thrived on ancestral nutrition – I want you to think of the hunter-gatherers of the past who ate a diet of quaility animal fats like bone marrow, fatty meats, eggs, seafood, and other organic in season fruits and veggies.

Unfortunately, in recent years we have seen a rise in processed foods, such as artificial sweeteners, processed sugars, grains and seed oils including Canola oil, palm oil, vegetable oil and many toxic seed oils.

These harmful ingredients are in the processed food we eat OR in our cooking oil that destroys the nutrients of our food. So having a diet removing these harmful processed ingredients is already a great first step.

These are the realculptrits behind the decline of our health. Over time consuming these ingredients can cause anything from autoimmune issues, obesity and many chronic diseases. That’s why it’s so important that we understand the changes in our food supply and approach this issue with an ancestral nutrition perspective.
So how can we apply ancestral tradition to improve our health in the modern sick world?

What would be the equivalent for todays version of an ancestral diet would suggest that you order grassfed and finished beef, animal fats, and organs from local regenerative farms, you eat wild caught fish and seafood, eggs and a few in season organic produce. It’s really simple. Keep it locally sourced and support your local farmers.

This is as close as we can get unless you have the capability to be come self sufficient and start your own homestead and take complete control of your own food supply.

It’s important to focus on nutrient-density and whole foods that are free of added sugar, grain, and seed oils.

When we first went carnivore for the first 2 years of our journey which has led us to now an ancestral animal based diet.... we often heard to argument that there was never any long term study conducted on the carnivore diet... or an all meat based diet.

It always made me think, well what were our ancestors eating for thousands of years??

Mainly Meat... and some in season fruits.

So we are not trying anything new or reinvent the wheel.... we are simply returning to traditional ways of living and eating.

Despite all of these technological advances in todays world, our overall health and wellness may actually be suffering due to this drastic shift in steering away from our ancestral diet.

This way of eating is rooted in our ancestral history and can offer a ton of healing benefits for those looking to improve their health with the food they eat. By embracing quality animal fats and eliminating processed foods and toxic seed oils, the Ancestral diet can help us unlock optimal health and enjoy the vibrant energy that comes from eating the way our ancestors did.
