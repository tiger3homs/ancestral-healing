# Performance Breakdown

the video performed exactly at channel average. Above 1.0 = outperformed.

tested these topics in long-form. The verdict there is *provisional*, not final.

| **20+ min** | **8** | **12,500** | **1.70×** |

**Long-form wins, clearly.** The 20+ minute bucket more than doubles the channel median.
The top two videos are 28 and 22 minutes. Yet only 8 of 120 videos are 20+ minutes —
this is the most obvious underexploited format on the channel.

The dip at 8–12 minutes is likely a content artifact (that bucket is dense with
mid-tier explainers) rather than a real duration penalty.

## By hook style

| Hook type | Videos | Median views | Ratio | Outliers |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **How-to / guide** | 7 | **7,600** | **1.03×** | 1 |
| Contrarian / myth-busting | 11 | 5,700 | 0.77× | 2 |
| Descriptive | 56 | 5,300 | 0.72× | 5 |
| ALL CAPS | 31 | 4,100 | 0.56× | 3 |
| Listicle (numbered) | 7 | 3,700 | 0.50× | 1 |
| Question | 8 | 3,100 | 0.42× | 0 |

Two things stand out:

1. **ALL-CAPS titles underperform** (0.56×) yet are used on 31 videos — the second
   most common style. This looks like a habit rather than a decision.
2. **Question titles are the weakest** (0.42×, zero outliers). "Is Too Much Red Meat
   Bad?" (1,800), "Is Meat Really Bad for Us?" (1,700), "Are Plant-Based Milks
   Healthy?" (1,500) all landed near the floor. Questions invite a scroll-past; the
   audience wants the answer in the title.

**How-to is the highest-performing style and is used only 7 times.**

## Format signal: shorts vs long-form

| | Median views | Views-to-subs ratio |
|---|---|---|
| Long-form | 5,300 | 0.25 |
| Shorts | 3,100 | 0.16 |

Long-form outperforms shorts on this channel on both measures. Shorts do have two
breakout hits (32,000 and 27,000), so the ceiling is there — but the *reliable*
returns are in long-form.

This matters because the AH Brain archive contains a lot of reel-format material
(`reel-ideas-for-circadian-health.md`, `circadian-content-ideas.md`) aimed at the
weaker format **and** the weaker topic simultaneously.
